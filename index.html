Your Journey

How Do I Become a Muslim?

If a servant accepts Islam and completes his Islam, Allah will record for him every good deed that he performed before [his Islam] — Sunan an-Nasa'i 4998

1

Learn & Reflect

Take your time exploring the core beliefs. Ask questions, read resources, and speak to someone knowledgeable.

2

Take the Shahadah

When you're ready, declare the testimony of faith — affirming belief in One God and Prophet Muhammad ﷺ.

3

Begin Practising

Start learning how to pray, the basics of wudu (ablution), and build your knowledge step by step.

4

Join the Community

Connect with mentors and fellow Muslims. We're here to support you every step of the way.
